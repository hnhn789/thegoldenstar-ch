# thegoldenstar-ch


This repository is a personal Chinese translation of "THE GOLDEN STAR -
A Mystic Crescendo in Twelve Visions
By J Michaud PhD"  and the originals were under the copyright of J Michaud PhD under the UK Copyright, Designs and Patents Act of 1988. 

The translation was intended for spreading Light to Chinese people, not for commercial use.
